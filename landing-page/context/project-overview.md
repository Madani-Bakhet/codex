# Codex: Project Overview

## 1. Project Goal & Mission
Codex is a premium software development agency providing top-tier solutions for web and mobile applications. Our mission is to handle all aspects of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) from ideation to deployment and maintenance. We deliver robust, scalable, and beautifully designed software tailored to our clients' needs.

## 2. Target Audience
Our target audience is extremely broad—essentially "everyone who needs software." This includes:
- **Visionary Founders & Startups:** Looking for an MVP or a scalable product to launch to market.
- **Small to Medium Enterprises (SMEs):** Needing digital transformation or internal tools.
- **Enterprise Corporations:** Requiring complex architectures, integrations, or dedicated development teams.

## 3. Site Architecture
The website is structured as a **Single-Page Application (SPA) landing page**. It consists of a single continuous flow of sections, ensuring high engagement without requiring the user to load new pages. Navigation relies on smooth scrolling to specific anchor IDs.

1. **Hero (`#home`)**
2. **Stats Counter**
3. **Tech Stack Marquee**
4. **Services (`#services`)**
5. **Our Process (`#process`)**
6. **Featured Work (`#portfolio`)**
7. **About & Team (`#about`)**
8. **Client Testimonials**
9. **FAQ Section (`#faq`)**
10. **Contact (`#contact`)**

## 4. Single-Page Content Strategy

### Hero Section (`#home`)
- **Headline:** "Building the Future of Software." High-tech animated background.
- **CTAs:** "Explore Our Services", "Let's Talk".

### Stats Counter
- **Metrics:** "100+ Projects Delivered", "99% Uptime", "24/7 Support". Animated numbers counting up on scroll.

### Tech Stack Marquee
- **Content:** An infinitely scrolling banner of technology logos (React, Next.js, Node, AWS, Docker, etc.) using modern SVG placeholders.

### Services (`#services`)
- **Web Development:** Highlight modern frameworks.
- **Mobile Development:** Cross-platform and native solutions.
- **SDLC & DevOps:** End-to-end lifecycle management.

### Our Process (`#process`)
- **Timeline:** Step-by-step interactive breakdown (Discovery → Design → Build → Launch).

### Featured Work (`#portfolio`)
- **Showcase:** A grid or carousel displaying our top recent projects with placeholder mockups and "View Case Study" buttons.

### About Us (`#about`)
- **Story:** How we started and our focus on high-quality code.
- **Team/Values:** Core values and interactive team member cards.

### Testimonials
- **Slider:** Positive feedback and success metrics from previous clients.

### FAQ Section (`#faq`)
- **Accordion:** Common questions regarding pricing, timelines, and post-launch support.

### Contact (`#contact`)
- **Header:** "Get in Touch."
- **Form:** Name, Email, Company, Project Description.
- **Alternative Contacts:** info@codex.com, Location details.
